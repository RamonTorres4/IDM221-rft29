Case Studies

Plastic-free Philly

Summer of 2023

During my time as the web and social media assistant, I was tasked with a project that would help launch the marketing campaign, plastic free philly. The project was far from simple. It involved 10 different artists with all different art styles. A video was created for each art installation which were around philadelphia. The goal of this project was to get people to reduce their use of single use plastic consumption.

My role in this was to work with our director of marketing to interview, film, and edit these artists to show their projects.

I worked with some really great artists with so many varying backgrounds. It was incredible to work with so many talented people and see what wonderful things they can do.

ANS Website Redesign

July-Current

I am working on a website redesign for the Academy of Natural Sciences of Drexel University's new site. I am doing this in a software called Sitecore Gold. This is a content management system which allows me to quickly and effeciently add or remove components depening on what is needed for specific pages.

My portion of the project is to manage and impement the different components to build a working site that helps accomplish the institution's mission. My tasks include the following:

  • Reading, understanding, and implementing components and templates according to the documentation.
  • Submitting bug tickets.
  • Selecting and uploading images, and writting alt text for each.
  • Reviewing pages imported from wordpress to check for any errors.
