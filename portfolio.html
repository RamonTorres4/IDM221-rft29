Diorama Renovation

In 2023, The Academy of Natural Sciences of Drexel University was able to clean and freshen up some of the dioramas that have been around since 1933. The photos that I have are from the finished and cleaned dioramas without the glass on. The glare with the lighting in the hall really kills a lot of good photos, so I made sure to get as many good photos as I could.

Members Night

Members' Night is a night that the Academy offers to it's members once a year. It's a fantastic event where the museum opens up the research areas and scientists can show off their incredible work and research to members.

I've worked Members' Night over the years, and I have captured some really great images that really shows off the collection. Here are some of my favorites.
